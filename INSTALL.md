# 📱 MinersWorldCoin (MWC) Android Miner Installation Guide

The **MWC Android Miner** allows anyone to mine MinersWorldCoin directly from their Android device. Follow these steps carefully to install and start mining.

---

## 1️⃣ Download the APK

1. Open your Android device’s browser.
2. Download the latest MWC Android Miner APK from GitHub:
   [Download MWC Android Miner v1.9.0](https://github.com/Miners-World-Coin-MWC/MinersWorldCoin-Android-Miner/releases/download/1.9.0/androidMinerMWC.apk)
3. Wait for the download to complete.

> ![Screenshot of Android Miner APK Download](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1442194072147067083/1477786148930851040/Screenshot_20260301_215335_Opera_GX.jpg?ex=69a60719\&is=69a4b599\&hm=7544c4a457b365d13e7264145be455a9a0bd5d01fac4f5d039cea8f2ad2af50c&=\&format=webp\&width=440\&height=953)

---

## 2️⃣ Allow Installation from Unknown Sources

Before installing APK files, your Android device needs permission to install apps from unknown sources.

1. Open **Settings** → **Security** or **Privacy**
2. Enable **Install unknown apps** for the browser or file manager you used to download the APK
3. Confirm any prompts

> ![Screenshot of Android Settings for Unknown Apps](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1442194072147067083/1477786337259294930/Screenshot_20260301_215508_Settings.jpg?ex=69a60746\&is=69a4b5c6\&hm=f16b0618975796531f32f067f2b7cdbc719d9affb089e4648cb59149dc8e115a&=\&format=webp\&width=440\&height=953)

---

## 3️⃣ Install the APK

1. Locate the downloaded `androidMinerMWC.apk` file in your device’s **Downloads** folder.
2. Tap the APK to open the **Package Installer**.
3. Review the permissions requested by the app.
4. Tap **Install**.

> ![Screenshot of Android Package Installer](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1442194072147067083/1477786251670065385/Screenshot_20260301_215449_Package_installer.jpg?ex=69a60732\&is=69a4b5b2\&hm=583f05c41e54b8390c1959e1b1c35d09b59fb419ed7b79d825646e40c0e52fd6&=\&format=webp\&width=440\&height=953)

---

## 4️⃣ Open the Miner App

1. Once installation is complete, tap **Open** to launch the MWC Android Miner.
2. You’ll see the welcome screen and mining interface.

> ![Screenshot of MWC Android Miner Launch](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1442194072147067083/1477786875455471708/Screenshot_20260301_215701_minersworldcoin_Android_Miner.jpg?ex=69a607c7\&is=69a4b647\&hm=5cc8f7d26390386d1e8bbc696630d15880b51685aa0f549df39b3096a425778d&=\&format=webp\&width=440\&height=953)

---

## 5️⃣ Configure the Miner

1. Enter your **MWC Wallet Address** where you want mining rewards to be sent.
2. Adjust the **CPU threads** you want to dedicate for mining.
3. Set any optional settings such as **start on boot** or **mining intensity**.

> ![Screenshot of Miner Settings](https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1442194072147067083/1477786545175134341/image0.jpg?ex=69a60778\&is=69a4b5f8\&hm=b8d155df444f6533fdcbf73b49ec72a1ab087b17f263d8f9555c4be3a1e849f0&=\&format=webp\&width=440\&height=953)

---

## 6️⃣ Start Mining

1. Tap **Start Mining**.
2. Monitor your hashrate and accepted shares on the dashboard.
3. Ensure your device stays connected and has sufficient battery/charging.

---

## ⚡ Tips & Recommendations

* **Use a charger** while mining to prevent battery drain.
* **Monitor device temperature**; reduce CPU threads if overheating.
* **Update regularly** — new releases are posted on [GitHub](https://github.com/Miners-World-Coin-MWC/MinersWorldCoin-Android-Miner/releases).
* **Always verify APK source** to avoid malicious copies.

---

This guide ensures **any user can install and start mining MWC on Android safely**.
