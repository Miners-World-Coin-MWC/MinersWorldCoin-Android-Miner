# Privacy Policy

**MinersWorldCoin Android Miner**

**Effective Date:** March 9, 2026

MinersWorldCoin Android Miner ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

The MinersWorldCoin Android Miner **does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data**.

Specifically, the App does **not collect**:

* Personal information
* Names
* Email addresses
* Phone numbers
* Location data
* Device identifiers
* Contacts
* Analytics data
* Usage statistics

The App operates entirely on your device and only performs cryptocurrency mining when started by the user.

---

## 2. Mining Configuration

When using the App, you may manually enter:

* A cryptocurrency wallet address
* A worker name
* Mining pool connection details

This information is **stored locally on your device only** and is **never transmitted to the developer or any third-party services**.

The information you provide is used solely to connect to the mining pool you select.

---

## 3. Network Communication

The App connects directly to mining pools chosen by the user in order to perform cryptocurrency mining.

These connections are **not controlled by the developer** of the App.

Any data exchanged during mining is strictly between your device and the mining pool you configure.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App **does not use third-party services** such as:

* Analytics platforms
* Advertising networks
* Tracking tools
* Data collection SDKs

---

## 5. Data Storage

All configuration settings remain **stored locally on your device**.

The developer of the App **does not have access to this information**.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the updated policy will be published with a revised effective date.
